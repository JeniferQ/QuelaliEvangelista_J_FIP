Dining Summary

Gastronomic 5 Course Meal

1 Amuse bouche, 2 Starters (pick a choice of Appy, Soup or Salad), 1 Main Entree Choice and 1 Dessert Choice.

All our room prices include Daily 5 Star, Classic 5 Course Gourmet Dinner in our European Style Dining Room or as a room service brought to your place, and Classic Style Breakfast In-Bed.

Vegan and Vegetarian dishes are available, we offer a large array of choices from Heart Smart Gluten Free and Celiac Disease on your request.
